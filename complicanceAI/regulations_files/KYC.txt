KYC Rules: 
#### Current Account
**Requirement: Proof of Identity**  
**Description:** Submit a valid Aadhaar card or PAN card.  
**Citation:** RBI Guidelines 2024, Section 16【4:4†source】.

**Requirement: Proof of Address**  
**Description:** Submit a recent utility bill or rent agreement.  
**Citation:** RBI Guidelines 2024, Section 16【4:4†source】.

**Requirement: Business Registration Documents**  
**Description:** Submit the certificate of incorporation and business registration documents.  
**Citation:** RBI Guidelines 2024, Section 17【17:17†source】.

#### NRI Accounts
**Requirement: Proof of Identity**  
**Description:** Submit a valid passport and visa.  
**Citation:** RBI Guidelines 2024, Section 44【6:6†source】.

**Requirement: Proof of Address**  
**Description:** Submit a recent utility bill or rent agreement from the home country.  
**Citation:** RBI Guidelines 2024, Section 44【6:6†source】.

**Requirement: Admission Letter**  
**Description:** For students, submit a letter offering admission from the educational institution in India.  
**Citation:** RBI Guidelines 2024, Section 44【6:6†source】.
