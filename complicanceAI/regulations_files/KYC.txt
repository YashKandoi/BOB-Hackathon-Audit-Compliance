KYC Rules: 
#### Current Account
**Requirement:** Proof of Identity  
**Description:** Submit a valid Aadhaar card or PAN card.  
**Citation:** RBI Guidelines, Section 16【16:51†source】

**Requirement:** Proof of Address  
**Description:** Submit a recent utility bill or rent agreement.  
**Citation:** RBI Guidelines, Section 16【16:51†source】

**Requirement:** Periodic Updation  
**Description:** Ensure periodic updation of KYC data as specified in Section 38 of the Master Direction.  
**Citation:** RBI Guidelines, Section 56【9:133†source】
