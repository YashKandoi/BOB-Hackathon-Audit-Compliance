KYC Rules: 
#### Current Account
1. **Requirement: Proof of Identity**
   - **Description:** Submit a valid Aadhaar card or PAN card.
   - **Citation:** RBI Guidelines 2024, Section 2.1【6:0†source】

2. **Requirement: Proof of Address**
   - **Description:** Submit a recent utility bill or rent agreement.
   - **Citation:** RBI Guidelines 2024, Section 2.2【6:0†source】

3. **Requirement: Business Registration Documents**
   - **Description:** Provide business registration documents such as Certificate of Incorporation.
   - **Citation:** RBI Guidelines 2024, Section 3.1【6:0†source】

1. **Requirement: Proof of Identity**
   - **Description:** Submit a valid passport.
   - **Citation:** RBI Guidelines 2024, Section 2.1【6:5†source】

2. **Requirement: Proof of Address**
   - **Description:** Submit a recent utility bill or rent agreement from the country of residence.
   - **Citation:** RBI Guidelines 2024, Section 2.2【6:5†source】

3. **Requirement: Overseas Address Proof**
   - **Description:** Provide proof of overseas address.
   - **Citation:** RBI Guidelines 2024, Section 3.4【6:5†source】
