KYC Rules: 
#### Individual Savings Account
**Requirement:** Proof of Identity  
**Description:** Submit a valid Aadhaar card or PAN card.  
**Citation:** RBI Guidelines 2024, Section 16【4:3†source】

**Requirement:** Proof of Address  
**Description:** Submit a recent utility bill or rent agreement.  
**Citation:** RBI Guidelines 2024, Section 16【4:3†source】

#### Current Account
**Requirement:** Certificate of Incorporation  
**Description:** Submit the certificate of incorporation for the business.  
**Citation:** RBI Guidelines 2024, Section 18【4:3†source】

**Requirement:** Board Resolution  
**Description:** Provide a board resolution authorizing the account opening.  
**Citation:** RBI Guidelines 2024, Section 18【4:3†source】

#### Salary Account
**Requirement:** Proof of Employment  
**Description:** Submit a letter from the employer or a recent payslip.  
**Citation:** RBI Guidelines 2024, Section 16【4:3†source】

**Requirement:** Proof of Identity  
**Description:** Submit a valid Aadhaar card or PAN card.  
**Citation:** RBI Guidelines 2024, Section 16【4:3†source】

#### Fixed Deposit Account
**Requirement:** Proof of Identity  
**Description:** Submit a valid Aadhaar card or PAN card.  
**Citation:** RBI Guidelines 2024, Section 16【4:3†source】

**Requirement:** Proof of Address  
**Description:** Submit a recent utility bill or rent agreement.  
**Citation:** RBI Guidelines 2024, Section 16【4:3†source】

#### Recurring Deposit Account
**Requirement:** Proof of Identity  
**Description:** Submit a valid Aadhaar card or PAN card.  
**Citation:** RBI Guidelines 2024, Section 16【4:3†source】

**Requirement:** Proof of Address  
**Description:** Submit a recent utility bill or rent agreement.  
**Citation:** RBI Guidelines 2024, Section 16【4:3†source】

#### NRI Accounts
**Requirement:** Passport  
**Description:** Submit a valid passport with visa and immigration endorsement.  
**Citation:** RBI Guidelines 2024, Section 44【4:7†source】

**Requirement:** Proof of Address in Home Country  
**Description:** Submit a utility bill or bank statement from the home country.  
**Citation:** RBI Guidelines 2024, Section 44【4:7†source】
