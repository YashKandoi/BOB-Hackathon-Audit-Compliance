KYC Rules: 
#### Current Account
**Requirement: Proof of Identity**
- **Description:** Submit a valid Aadhaar card or PAN card.
- **Citation:** RBI Guidelines 2024, Section 16【4:6†source】

**Requirement: Proof of Address**
- **Description:** Submit a recent utility bill or rent agreement.
- **Citation:** RBI Guidelines 2024, Section 16【4:6†source】

**Requirement: Customer Due Diligence (CDD)**
- **Description:** Obtain the Aadhaar number and PAN or Form No. 60.
- **Citation:** RBI Guidelines 2024, Section 16【4:6†source】

#### NRI Accounts
**Requirement: Proof of Identity**
- **Description:** Submit a valid passport with visa and immigration endorsement.
- **Citation:** RBI Guidelines 2024, Section 44【4:11†source】

**Requirement: Proof of Address**
- **Description:** Submit proof of address in the home country along with a photograph and a letter offering admission from the educational institution in India.
- **Citation:** RBI Guidelines 2024, Section 44【4:11†source】

**Requirement: Customer Due Diligence (CDD)**
- **Description:** Obtain a declaration about the local address within 30 days of opening the account.
- **Citation:** RBI Guidelines 2024, Section 44【4:11†source】
